A Glimpse of SMK Negeri 3 Kendal

General Information

SMK Negeri 3 Kendal continues to strive to produce graduates in three BMW categories (Working, continuing to higher education, entrepreneurship). The school, which was established in the 2004/2005 academic year, has 5 areas of expertise: Automotive Engineering (TO), Computer Network and Telecommunication Engineering (TJKT), Software and Game Development (PPLG), Electronics Engineering, and Industrial Chemical Engineering. All of these areas of expertise have been accredited A. The school is located in Salamsari Village, Boja District, Kendal Regency, with a land area of 30,000 m² and a building area of 2,485 m².

Registrant Enthusiasm

The condition of new student applicants at SMK Negeri 3 Kendal from the first year until the 2023 / 2024 academic year has always experienced changes. This has impacted the increase in the number of classes / study groups.

Vocational Program of SMK Negeri 3 Kendal

Automotive Engineering

A major that studies vehicle engines, ranging from the basics of engines, engine maintenance, to engine repair.

Electronic Engineering

A major that studies weak electric currents, semiconductor devices, and electrical components.

Industrial Chemical Engineering

A major that studies the process of processing raw materials into finished products, from design, operation, to process control.

Software Development and GIM

A major that studies computer programming, whether for desktop software, mobile, or games.

Computer Network and Telecommunications Engineering

A major that studies computer networks, computer network hardware and software, as well as telecommunications.
